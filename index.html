We Are The First Argentine Company To Bring Floors From Germany

Berlin Planks was born in 2011 as the first company dedicated exclusively to Floors. We started with Floating Floors, and little by little we incorporated Vinyl Floors with inserts, WPC Decks, Porcelain Tiles and Prefinished. We are a company inspired by providing the best service and quality, just like European companies, where the customer comes first. The customer who enters Berlin Planks quickly finds an advisor who will accompany him throughout the purchase process until after-sales. We have more than 300 different colors and designs of Floors, since we always seek the forefront of world trends and meet the tastes of each of our clients. Our philosophy is customer-focused. Both our products and premises as well as our employees and installers have a single objective: customer satisfaction.
