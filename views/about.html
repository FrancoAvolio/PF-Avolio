Our Company

Our vision is to be the best and most complete flooring company in Argentina. Every year we visit the most important flooring fairs in the world: Domotex Germany, Domotex Shanghai, Cevisama Valencia, Cersaie Bologna. What we are looking for with this is to have the most modern products and colors, following world trends. In this way, we were the first in the market to bring Vinyl Floors with inserts and WPC Decks. Our customers will find the latest products and colors. Our mission is to bring all these products to your home, taking care of quality, advice, training installers, so that this whole process becomes an easy and pleasant shopping experience.
